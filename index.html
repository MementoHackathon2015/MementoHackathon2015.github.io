The Memento Hackathon is an event to learn about web archiving and long term preservation strategies. During these two days, Ghent University invited Herbert Van de Sompel, Los Alamos National Laboratory, as speaker about his Memento web archiving project: http://timetravel.mementoweb.org/

The Hackathon will bring together digital library enthusiasts, programmers and web archiving specialists to discuss long term preservation strategies and get hands on experience with Memento.
